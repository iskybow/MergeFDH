how does it work?

Being our client, you delegate all your staff's tasks to our remote employees - dispatching, call answering, remote operations and duties related to your call center company needs. We take care of the equipment setup and online operator’s work coordination. We provide you with a detailed report on the work that has been done and the tasks that have been completed. Our company has developed its own remote employee resources management model to provide an excellent final result.

1 stepIntroduction

You give us information about exactly what you need. We analyze every detail and work on creating our working plan, involving the team in the process.

2 stepDevelopment plan

We create a development project “plan”. We select your remote professionals, train them specifically to fit your requirements, and familiarize them with the tasks of your company, that they will be outsourcing.

3 stepTeam working

We schedule an interview to introduce you to your new staff and start the free training - we learn the tasks and working processes during the 7-day period with you and select the most appropriate employees by your feedback

4 stepStart saving up to 65%!

From this day on, you start saving on a wide list of office and HR expenses by up to 65%, just by outsourcing positions or even whole business processes that are not of core importance for your company.

5 stepSuccessfull cooperation

We provide you with a daily/ weekly report and analyze the efficiency of our remote employee working process. Enjoy the results!
