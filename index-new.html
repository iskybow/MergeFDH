WHAT IS A REMOTE EMPLOYEE?

Remote employees - are professionals working outside of a traditional office environment. The main concept is: the work does not need to be done in a specific physical place (your office) to be accomplished successfully. Doing the work remotely with Front Desk Helpers can bring significant benefits to any size companies. And all this because you are saving instantly on many general office and HR expenses, and have a skilled employee taking care of your jobs, the same ways your ground office staff do, but in a more productive way due to the comfortability of working conditions, and access to all necessary tools provided by you and yours.

The only difference between the remote employee and your office staff is that now you work together online using the latest technologies, apps, and platforms for virtual work: chats and communication systems, professional telecommunication base stations, CRM and Project Management cloud services, and so on.
